Light Restoration

Headlight Restoration is a professional service designed to restore the clarity and brilliance of your car’s headlights. Over time, exposure to sunlight, UV rays, oxidation, and road debris causes headlights to turn yellow, foggy, or scratched. Our restoration process not only enhances the aesthetic appeal of your vehicle but is essential for your safety by significantly improving nighttime visibility.

Our Restoration Process

  • Deep Cleaning: Removal of surface oxidation and yellowing layers.
  • Multi-stage Sanding: Precise wet sanding to eliminate deep scratches.
  • Polishing & Buffing: Machine polishing to bring back factory-level clarity.
  • UV Protection: Application of a protective coating to prevent future fading.
  • Safety Guaranteed: Restores maximum brightness for safer night driving.

Why It’s Important

Cloudy headlights reduce light output, making it difficult to see the road ahead and making your car less visible to others. A professional restoration ensures long-lasting clarity, maintains your vehicle’s resale value, and provides the peace of mind that comes with superior visibility on the road.
